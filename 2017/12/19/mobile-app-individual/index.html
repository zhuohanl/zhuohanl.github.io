A self-developed Mobile App

This is a mobile application for the Carnegie Mellon University Australia (CMUA)’s library system.

This app serves:
• The public who is interested in knowing what books are available in CMUA’s library;
• CMUA students and staff (more importantly).

This app will cover basic functions including:
• Search & find book (major function);
• Library opening hour, address, contact, etc.;
• Frequently asked questions;
• Personal library records.
